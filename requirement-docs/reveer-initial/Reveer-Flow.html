Design decisions
Route status badge
The only explicit novelty signal shown to the user. "New route" appears in blue on the normal preview screen; "Previously used route" appears in grey on the edge case screen. No further explanation is given — the variation is otherwise silent.
History commits at "Start"
"Try a different route" can be tapped any number of times without affecting stored history. History is only written the moment the user taps "Start in Google Maps" — not before, not after. Reveer has no visibility into the trip once Google Maps takes over.
Tolerance lives in Settings only
Time tolerance is not exposed on the route preview screen. Users who want to adjust it must go to Settings from the home screen before entering a destination. This keeps the preview screen focused on the single decision: start or try again.
